William Shakespeare Competition

An introduction to his life, times, and works through a game.

Game Rules

Overview: In three rounds, you will gather facts about William Shakespeare.

  • Round 1: The Times of Shakespeare
  • Round 2: The Life of Shakespeare
  • Round 3: The Works of Shakespeare

Each round has two phases:

  • Phase 1: You have 5 minutes to use computers to gather facts for the round's topic.
  • Phase 2: Take turns sharing one fact. Cross off facts given by other teams.
    • You earn 1 point per unique fact given!
    • One team may steal a point if they have more relevant detail about the same fact
